Find out How To Protect Yourself From Fraud

Identity Theft:
  • Stealing Someone's Identity To Obtain Insurance Policies
  • Using Someone Else's Personal Information Without Their Permission Or Knowledge
  • The Fraudulent Practice Of Using Another Person's Name And Personal Information In Order To Obtain Credit, Loans, Etc
How To Protect Yourself:
  • Give Your Personal Details To Anyone No Matter Stranger Or Known
  • Password-Protect Your Devices
  • Collect Your Mail Every Day And Place A Hold On Your Mail When You Will Be On Vacation Or Away From Your Home
