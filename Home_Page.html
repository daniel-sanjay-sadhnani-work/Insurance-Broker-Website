Insurance Plans

Daniel Insurance is an Insurance Recommendation Website that helps people find insurance schemes that is best suited for them. We have a wide variety of insurance schemes that people can choose with the help of a questionnare that informs our customers whether it is suitable or not based on their responses. if your insurance scheme choice is deemed suitable , customers will proceed to make payment whereas if your insurance scheme choice is deemed not suitable , customers will have to make another insurance scheme choice.

Fraud Protection

Daniel Insurance provides a Fraud Protection message which customers can access and Read to understand how to protect themselves. it includes Identity fraud which is the most common fraud scheme. it shares the 3 most common causes of identity fraud and the 3 ways to help protect customers from Identity fraud.

Transaction History

Daniel Insurance provides a transaction history table of a customer which helps us detect Money Laundering based on their payment details. Based On acustomers transactions in the past month , the table will show its status to not only the customers , but to us as well. With the use of icons , green indicating low risk of money laundering , orange indicating moderate risk and red indicating high risk where we will be filing STRs if any high or moderate risk is detected.
